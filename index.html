Why Billboards?Credibility and repetition.

In a competitive and rapidly changing world and marketplace, billboards are there day in and day out. Viewers can’t change the channel, opt out, unsubscribe, or throw it away. Additionally, they don’t require active participation to be viewed; no need to click, turn the page, or buy a subscription. They are just out there, and for most viewers day after day after day.

Locally the Inland Empire (composed of San Bernardino and Riverside Counties and also referred to the Riverside-San Bernardino-Ontario CBSA) is a culture of drivers because it is so big (over 27,000 sq.mi.) but has very limited public transportation options. Statistically over 90% of Inland Empire residents drive to work, and over 45% of them commute an hour or more each day.
